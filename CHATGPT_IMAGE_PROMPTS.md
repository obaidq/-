## ChatGPT Tips
1. **Fresh chat per category** (mascot, backgrounds, patterns)
2. **Pin the falcon style:** After prompt 1, say *"Remember this character for all future images"*
3. **Transparent bg:** Say *"Make the background transparent"*
4. **Too realistic?** Add *"cartoon, flat colors, bold outlines, cel-shaded"*
5. **Too busy?** Add *"clean composition, negative space, minimal"*
6. **Refine:** Say *"Same but darker"* or *"More neon glow"*
7. **Download PNG** for transparency, WebP/JPG for backgrounds
